Badrinath Temple

About

It is a Religious Shrine of Lord Vishnu & One of Most Religious Pilgrimage in Char Dham Yatra. It is Located in Chamoli District of Uttarakhand at a elevation of 3,133 mts. We at Uttarakhand Holidays provide all Kind of Services During Badrinath Yatra to the Pilgrimages.

How to get there

Around 317 km away is the Jolly Grant Airport near Dehradun. Flights from several major Indian cities land here. The major cities of north India are well-connected to Badrinath. One can use buses that ply regularly from Delhi, Haridwar and Rishikesh to Badrinath.
